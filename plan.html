Tour Plan

Plan your trip day-by-day with our curated travel itinerary designed for comfort, exploration, and local experiences.

Day 1: Arrival & Local Welcome

Arrive at the destination and settle into your homestay. Enjoy a traditional welcome drink, followed by a cultural introduction and local dinner.

Day 2: Village Tour & Nature Walk

Take a guided tour of the nearby village and participate in local activities like pottery or farming. In the evening, enjoy a scenic nature walk to the nearby hills.

Day 3: Local Cuisine & Handicrafts

Learn to cook authentic local dishes in the morning. In the afternoon, visit markets or local artisans and explore regional crafts.

Day 4: Adventure & Departure

Optional activities like ziplining or river rafting depending on location. Wrap up with a group breakfast and depart with memories and souvenirs.
